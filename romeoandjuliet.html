Summary

The story begins with the Capulets and the Mountages fighting in the plaza, the prince came and was upset by the situation so he ordered peace between the families. If that happened again they will be condemned to death.

Romeo was really melancholic because he was in love with Rosaline. His friends decided to take him to the Capulets masked ball. They were not welcome there because they were Mountages, but they went anyways. In there, Romeo fell in love with Juliet, a really beautiful girl daughter of Lord Capulet. After the masked ball, Juliet's mother asked Juliet if she would agree to marry Count Paris, a really rich and powerful man from Verona. In the ball, Tybalt heard Romeo's voice and he realised that he was a Mountage, so he challenged him to a duel the next morning. After the ball, Romeo went to Juliet's room and he kissed her.

The next morning, Romeo asked Friar Laurence if he could marry him with Juliet. He agreed after asking how in love were them. Romeo's servant told his friends that Tybalt had challenged him to a duel, but it never happened.

Romeo told Juliet's nurse that they were marrying. After the marriage, Romeo fought Tybalt and ended up killing him after Tybalt killed Mercutio. The prince condemned Romeo to death.

Juliet was informed that Romeo was exiled and she cried for days. He tried to kilk himself. The Capulets organised Juliet's wedding with Count Paris. Romeo went to Juliet's balcony to say goodbye. Juliet is told that she is going to marry Paris. She goes to Friar Laurcence's and tells him the news and he makes a plan to avoid this wedding and allow Juliet to live her love with Romeo without having to worry about their families hating each other.

The plan goes wrong and Romeo and Juliet end up commiting suicide. Because of this, their families finally stopped hating each other.
